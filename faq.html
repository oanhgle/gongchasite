Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question?


    General Questions

    + How can I order?

    Come to our shop or call us! We currently do not fulfill online orders or have delivery services.

    You can customize what size, ice level, sugar level, and toppings you would like for each drink. If you prefer drinks that are less sweet, we recommend ordering 30% or 50% sugar because we can always add more sugar to your drink after you try it!

    + When are you open? Do you accept phone/online orders?

    We are open everyday from 11 AM to 9 PM and we accept phone orders! Drinks will be fully completed once they are paid for in-person at the shop, and food will be made a few minutes before your arrival to ensure freshness.

    Please note that we do not offer delivery services (ourselves or through any services such as Grubhub), so orders will have to be picked up from our store.

    + How does your rewards system work? How can I sign up?

    Every time you order, please input your phone number or email address. Once you have bought ten drinks with us, you'll get a free medium no topping drink.

    Please use the Clover app or present your QR code to the cashier to redeem.

    + Do you sell any of the drinks in bulk/in gallons?

    We currently do not, but please feel free to call the shop in advance and ask as this may be fulfilled on a case-by-case basis.

    + Do you have any store vouchers or gift cards?

    We do not offer any store vouchers or gift cards at the moment.

    + What is the best way to provide feedback, ask questions, make menu requests, or address complaints?

    Please reach out to us via our email (gongchatampa@gmail.com) or message us on Instagram (@gongchatampa); this is also the best method of communication for business inquiries, and we will try to respond within 24 hours. We're grateful for all customer feedback!

    Drink Questions

    + What is boba?

    Boba is made from tapioca, which is a gluten-free starch extracted from the root of a cassava plant. By combining the tapioca starch with brown sugar syrup, water, potassium sorbate and guar gum, you get flavorless chewy black pearls suitable for any drink. We offer a variety of toppings for your drinks and waffles for you to try.

    + What is the difference between black boba and white boba?

    Black pearls are sweeter, but boba is mostly flavorless.

    + What if I want to save my drink for the next day?

    If you want to save your drink for the next day, we recommend ordering 0% or 30% ice as to lessen the dilution; please keep in mind that 0% ice costs an extra $0.75 per cup since you get significantly more tea this way! We also recommend getting white pearls over black pearls. Black tapioca pearls tend to harden when refrigerated.

    + Is boba gluten-free?

    The boba pearls are gluten-free. For gluten-free drinks, please ask the cashier.

    + Do you guys have an original or signature milk tea?

    Yes! The original milk tea (known as the signature milk tea) is just milk tea made with black tea! We offer most of our drinks with black tea, green tea, oolong tea, or Earl Grey tea.

    + Does your creamer have dairy? Do you have any drinks without dairy?

    Our plant-based creamer powder and some of the other drink powders contain dairy. If you are looking for non-dairy drinks, we recommend the brewed teas or Wintermelon drinks (aside from the Wintermelon Milk Drink)!

    + Do you have any non-dairy alternatives?

    Yes, we have soy milk and almond milk - just ask for it!

    + Do you have decaf tea or caffeine-free options?

    Unfortunately, we do not currently have decaf tea. We do have caffeine-free options, which are denoted on our in-store menu with two asterisks (**).

    + Do you use honey for your black tapioca pearls?

    Nope, we use brown sugar.

    + What types of coffee do you have?

    We have milk coffee, coffee milk tea, and brown sugar coffee milk tea. For these drinks, we can use regular coffee or strong coffee - please specify which you prefer upon ordering. We have Vietnamese coffee (which is the Milk Coffee with strong coffee). We do not currently have café con leche.

    + What are the differences between all of the Brown Sugar drinks?

    Our Fresh Milk drinks use milk straight out of the refridgerator.

    The Brown Sugar Fresh Milk has no tea. The Brown Sugar Fresh Milk Tea is mostly milk with tea poured in at the end and brown sugar drizzled around the cup. The Brown Sugar Milk Tea has milk tea with brown sugar.

    + Do you guys have _ drink/topping?

    We are continuously adding new drinks and toppings to our menu; if you ask, we can try to make it if we have the ingredients on hand or consider adding it to our menu in the future!

    Food Questions

    + What are bubble waffles? What do they taste like?

    A bubble waffle is a Chinese sweet and custardy snack made in a special waffle iron. It includes eggs, flour, milk and sugar. We also have a variety of toppings and ice cream for them. The toppings can be put inside of or on top of the waffle; we can also store them in a little container for you to take home if desired. Bubble waffles are easy to eat alone or with friends - just break the little bubbles off! They take us about 5 - 8 minutes to make.

    + How can we pay for the items on the Daily Special menu?

    We accept cash only for the items on our Daily Special menu. We have cash on hand as change for larger bills.

    + Can I customize how I want my foods and drinks from the Daily Special menu?

    Basic requests, such as no pepper, for food can be done. While we have some additional spices and condiments, our range is limited to what you would find in a typical Vietnamese kitchen.

    As with drinks from our regular menu, the sugar and ice levels can be adjusted as desired. The menu price comes with one topping.

    Please keep in mind that the wait time for the Daily Special menu varies by item, but we will always work diligently to get your order out in a timely manner.

    + What is in the bánh mì thịt nguội?

    Ingredients include pickled vegetables, cucumber, pâté, butter, and pork.

    + Are there any vegetarian food options from the Daily Special Menu?

    Unfortunately, we currently do not have any aside from the xà lách trộn (Caesar salad) which can be made with or without meat.
